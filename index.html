About Lightbearers

Lightbearers is a ministry dedicated to supporting children through mentorship, Bible learning, education support and community activities. Our goal is to give every child a safe environment where they can grow spiritually, socially and academically.

Lightbearers Academy

Located at 34 Jacaranda Street, New Town, Luanshya, Lightbearers Academy will offer affordable private education for children from Kindergarten to Grade 5.

Kindergarten

Learning through play, creativity and early reading.

Primary Education

Grade 1 to Grade 5 focusing on literacy, numeracy and personal growth.

Christian Values
